Available Transactions

Welcome to Hbank, your reliable and modern banking solution designed to simplify your financial transactions. At Hbank, we understand the importance of secure, efficient, and user-friendly banking services. Our mission is to provide you with the essential banking functions you need, without the hassle. At HBank, we currently offer deposit, withdraw and transfer functions. Bare with us as we upgrade to full scale soon.

DEPOSIT

To deposit with our console, use:
deposit <customer acct num> <amt>

WITHDRAW

To withdraw funds from your account using our console, use:
withdraw <customer acct num> <amt> <passwowrd>

TRANSFER

To transfer funds to another customer, use:
transfer <sender's acct num> <receiver's acct num> <amt> <sender's password>
